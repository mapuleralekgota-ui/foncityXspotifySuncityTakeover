About Suncity Takeover
The Sun City Takeover is HCE’s 4-day destination event. Carefully curated to
provide patrons with a combination of a travelling experience as well as
entertainment value. The Takeover speaks directly to the need for young
professionals to travel with friends and engage in exciting new experiences,
hosted at the Sun City Resort.
With the launch in 2020, the Takeover is the most anticipated experience by
HCE audiences. Over 5 days, HCE is transported to Sun City Resort taking our
resident DJs and event properties with us. The weekend also consists of a
TshwaneFontein, fondly dubbed “Fon City”. 
In 2025, the event sold out in one hour, which shows the popularity of the
event with our loyal patrons. Sun City Takeover packages range from R13 500 -
R40 000 depending on the hotel.
Dates: 6 November - 9 November 2026
Format: 4 - day experience
Attendees: 3500 pax 
Gender Split estimate: 60% women and 40% men
Venue: Sun City Resort
Region: North West
Target Consumer: 23 - 45

RUN  & PADEL CLUB 
Run and Padel Club offers partners a unique opportunity to sponsor two standout experiences at the Sun City Takeover. These curated
moments are designed to bring together a community of individuals who are passionate about health, wellness and an active lifestyle. By
aligning with these experiences, partners can engage directly with a highly relevant and receptive audience in an authentic, high-energy
environment.
Run and Padel Club Naming Rights : Partners have the exclusive opportunity to
secure naming rights for the Run Club, Padel Club, or both—placing their brand at
the forefront of these signature experiences (eg. BrandX Padel Club or Padel
Club powered by BrandX). This premium positioning ensures consistent visibility
across all event touchpoints, from pre-event promotion to on-site branding and
post-event content.
Run and Padel Club Activations: Partners also have the opportunity to activate
within the Run Club and Padel Club, creating immersive brand experiences that
go beyond visibility. Through on-the-ground engagement—such as branded
water points, product sampling, interactive touchpoints or curated moments
woven into the program—partners can connect with attendees in a meaningful
and memorable way.

Lead Sponsor Opportunities
POURING:PREMIUM HIGH ENGAGEMENT
Sponsorship Tier: Pouring Rights
Total Category Dominance. This tier is designed for alcohol partners who want to ensure their product is the only choice for patrons across the entire Sun City
Takeover.
The two available categories are:
1.Lead Spirits Partner: Exclusive rights for all spirits (Vodka, Gin, Tequila, Whiskey, etc.).
2.Lead Beers & Ciders Partner: Exclusive rights for all malt-based beverages and ciders.
Key Highlights
Category Exclusivity: Once a brand secures a category, we "lock the doors" to competitors. If you are the Spirit Sponsor, your portfolio is the only spirit
selection available at every bar, party and Day Club unless an agreement for specific product line only has been made.
Guaranteed Stock Procurement (Buy-in): Your products will be the mandatory house-pour on every menu across the entire festival weekend.
Menu Integration & Visibility: Your brand will receive primary placement on all physical and digital bar menus. 
Point-of-Sale (POS) Dominance: Sponsors have the opportunity to brand the service bar areas, including branded glassware (or high-quality disposables),
Fridges and back-bar displays.
The "Takeover" Effect: Because the festival spans multiple events across the property, your brand achieves massive "liquid-to-lips" reach, ensuring every
attendee interacts with your product multiple times a day.
Pouring Rights Sponsor
The Exclusive Beverage Partner Reserved specifically for alcohol brands, this tier offers a dominant market position throughout the festival.
Category Exclusivity & Stock Buy-in: Enjoy exclusive rights within your specific category (e.g., Spirits, or Beers & Ciders) and guaranteed
volume sales
