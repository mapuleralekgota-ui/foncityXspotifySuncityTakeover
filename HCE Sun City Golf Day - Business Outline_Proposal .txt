EXECUTIVE SUMMARY: THE VISION, THE IMPACT, 
THE OPPORTUNITY
Partnering with Homecoming Foundation and Lads On Tour TV, the HCE Sun City Golf Day 2026 is designed as a premium fundraisin g experience. We will host a 
selective field of high caliber golfers on the Lost City Championship Course to drive fundraising, brand exposure and lasting  community impact.
EVENT SCALE
The event will host 88 to 100 golfers at the prestigious Lost 
City Golf Course, supported by sponsors, invited guests, 
media teams and event staff, creating a premium full -day 
experience.
STRATEGIC TIMING
Opening event of the 3-day HCE Sun City Takeover 
(6 – 8 Nov 2026). Strategically launches the HCE Sun City 
Takeover with premium energy, early sponsor visibility and 
strong networking momentum.
CORE MISSION
The core mission of the Sun City Takeover Golf Day is to unite 
premium experiences, meaningful networking and philanthropic 
impact to raise funds for the HomeComing Foundation, enabling 
the upliftment of schools, the dignified enhancement of 
sanitation and essential learning facilities, and the advancement 
of community development and social equity programmes that 
create sustainable change. Through this platform, corporates, 
institutions, players and partners are invited to move beyond 
sponsorship and play an active role in transforming communities, 
expanding opportunity and leaving a measurable legacy that 
extends far beyond the event itself.
AUDIENCE PROFILE
Upwardly mobile audience of professionals, entrepreneurs, 
executives, business owners and socially influential 
consumers who value quality experiences. Predominantly 
aged between 28 and 50.
EXECUTION EXCELLENCE
Professional coordination by Lads On Tour TV for a world -
class experience, driven through military -grade planning,

Lads On Tour TV.

THE ARENA: LOST CITY 
GOLF COURSE & EVENT 
MECHANICS
The Lost City Golf Course at Sun City Resort is a desert-style championship masterpiece, 
renowned for its dramatic undulating terrain, immaculate fairways and breathtaking 
bushveld vistas. This iconic 18-hole venue provides an exclusive, world-class setting 
befitting a premium flagship event of this caliber.
A competitive field of 88 to 100 players will participate in a Stableford format with an 
85% handicap allowance, while live digital scoring through Score Capture will deliver a 
dynamic, real-time leaderboard experience that keeps competition engaging 
throughout the day.
ASPECT DETAIL
Venue Lost City Golf Course, Sun City Resort
Player Capacity 88 - 100 Golfers (22-25 Four-balls)
Format Individual Stableford (IPS), 85% Handicap Allowance
Handicap Verification Official HNA handicaps required; non-registered 
players play off a course handicap of 18.0
Scoring System Live digital scoring via Score Capture mobile app and 
devices, provided by the Score Capture team.
Course Highlights Iconic 13th Hole (Crocodile Pit), desert-style 
landscaping, panoramic views
